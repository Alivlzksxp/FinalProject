About Us

At PetPals, we believe every pet deserves a loving home

PetPals is a platform that serves as a bridge between you and these deserving pets!

We are dedicated to connecting loving homes with pets in need of adoption and currently partnering with Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS).

Our Story

Founded in 2024, PetPals was born out of passion for animal welfare. Our team recognized the need for a comprehensive, user-friendly platform that bridges the gap between shelters, rescues, and potential pet owners.

Our Team

We started this journey with a simple goal: to create a centralized hub where adopters can easily browse, filter, and find pets that match their preferences. By collaborating with local shelters and rescue organizations, we provide a wide array of pets ready for adoption, ensuring you have the best chance to find your perfect companion.

Currently, we are proud to partner with the Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS), a volunteer-based, non-government organization whose goal is to prevent animal cruelty through education, animal sheltering and advocacy. By working with PAWS, we ensure that all pets listed on our platform are well-cared for and ready to find their forever homes.
