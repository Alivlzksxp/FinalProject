About Us

At PetPals, we believe every pet deserves a loving home

PetPals is a platform that serves as a bridge between you and these deserving pets!

We are dedicated to connecting loving homes with pets in need of adoption and currently partnering with Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS).

Our Story

Founded in 2024, PetPals was born out of passion for animal welfare. Our team recognized the need for a comprehensive, user-friendly platform that bridges the gap between shelters, rescues, and potential pet owners.

How you can help

Adopt, Donate, Volunteer

Adopt a sheltered cat or dog to give them the opportunity to find a new home and enjoy the love and security they deserve.

You can still help without adopting by donating to fund the care of other animals in need.

While not everyone can adopt or foster, there are always alternative ways to help by volunteering.
